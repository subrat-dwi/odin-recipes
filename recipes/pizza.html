Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish featuring a flat, round dough base topped with a rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and various toppings like pepperoni, vegetables, or herbs. Baked until crispy, it combines savory flavors and a chewy texture, making it a versatile and customizable meal enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup warm wate
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, bell peppers, olives, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix yeast, warm water, and sugar; let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy. In a bowl, combine flour and salt, then add yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead into a smooth dough. Cover and let rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  2. Shape Dough: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Add Toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough, sprinkle with cheese, and add desired toppings.
  4. Bake: Transfer pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  5. Enjoy: Serve hot and enjoy!
